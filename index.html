How I Work

I’ve used tools like Photoshop and Figma> for some personal projects and some graphic design. I enjoy turning ideas into visuals and seeing how they come together.

Right now, I’m taking SEG3125: Analysis and Design of User Interfaces at the University of Ottawa, where we’re learning things like visual design, usability, and user-centered thinking. I’ve also been exploring extra resources like the Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g) to get a better feel for what makes a solid user experience. This portfolio is me trying to put all of that into practice.
